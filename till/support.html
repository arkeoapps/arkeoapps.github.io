FAQ

Q. How do I create a new event?

Tap the + button on the top right of the Timeline or Events screen. Enter a title, choose a date and color, then tap Save.

Q. What is "Count days since" mode?

When enabled, the event counts up from a past date instead of counting down. Perfect for tracking anniversaries or milestones.

Q. What does Till Pro include?

Till Pro unlocks premium themes, additional footprint styles, and removes the event limit. It's a one-time purchase with no subscription.

Q. Is my data backed up?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. iCloud sync is planned for a future update. We recommend regular device backups via iCloud or iTunes.

Q. Can I use Till on iPad?

Yes! Till is fully optimized for both iPhone and iPad.

System Requirements

  • iOS 18.0 or later
  • iPhone and iPad compatible
  • Approximately 15 MB of storage
