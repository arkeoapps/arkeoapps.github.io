Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Till does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1 Information We Collect

Till is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information.

  • No account registration required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No server-side data storage

2 Data Storage

All event data (titles, dates, colors, categories) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

If you enable iCloud sync in a future update, data will be stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy policies. We never have access to your iCloud data.

3 Third-Party Services

Till does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

The app may connect to the App Store for in-app purchases (Till Pro). These transactions are handled entirely by Apple.

4 Children's Privacy

Till does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

5 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
