FAQ

Q. Does NoteVox require an internet connection?

No. NoteVox uses WhisperKit, an on-device AI engine, to transcribe speech. All processing happens locally on your device — even in airplane mode.

Q. Which languages are supported?

NoteVox supports 100+ languages through the Whisper AI model, including English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, and many more.

Q. How does iCloud sync work?

With iCloud enabled, your recordings and transcriptions automatically sync across your Apple devices. All data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account.

Q. Is my audio data private?

Yes. All audio processing happens on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your audio recordings or transcriptions on any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q. Can I import audio files from other apps?

Yes. NoteVox supports Share Extension — you can send audio files from Files, Voice Memos, or any other app directly to NoteVox for transcription.

Q. What AI model does NoteVox use?

NoteVox uses WhisperKit with OpenAI's Whisper large-v3 model, optimized to run entirely on Apple Silicon. No cloud processing required.

System Requirements

  • iOS 26.0 or later
  • iPhone and iPad compatible
  • Approximately 700 MB of storage (includes AI model)
  • Apple Silicon recommended for best performance
