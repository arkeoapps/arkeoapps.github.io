Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

NoteVox processes all audio on-device. We never collect, transmit, or store your data.

1 Information We Collect

NoteVox is built with an offline-first, privacy-first architecture. We do not collect any personal information.

  • No account registration required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No server-side data storage or processing

2 Audio & Transcription Data

All audio recordings are stored locally on your device in the app's sandboxed container.

Speech-to-text transcription is performed entirely on-device using WhisperKit, an on-device AI engine. No audio data is ever sent to external servers.

If you enable iCloud sync, your recordings and transcriptions are stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy policies. We never have access to your iCloud data.

3 Microphone Access

NoteVox requires microphone access solely for recording audio. The microphone is only active when you explicitly start a recording. Audio data never leaves your device.

4 Third-Party Services

WhisperKit: On-device speech recognition engine by Argmax. All processing is local — no data is sent to external servers.

Apple iCloud: Optional cloud sync through your personal Apple account, governed by Apple's privacy policy.

NoteVox does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

5 Children's Privacy

NoteVox does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

6 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
